Setting up a personal account

Choose one of the Local schemes

The limited Local Resident’s Discount Scheme

Costs £10 per year for the first 50 trips. After those trip additional ones cost £0.20p and you can provide payment details for this below. We will tell you when you have 5 trips remaining from your £10.

The unlimited Local Resident’s Scheme

The unlimited Local Resident’s Discount Scheme costs £20 per year for unlimited trips.

If you make less than 100 trips per year you should consider the limited option.
If you make more than 100 trips per year you should consider the unlimited option.
